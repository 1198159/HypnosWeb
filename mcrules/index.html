Rules

*Please Note: This Website Is Still Under Development*

The Rules:

1) Please respect all community members.

2) Do not spam, people have conversations from time to time.

3) Do not randomly ping people.

4) Please post in respective channels.

5) Do not ask to be promoted in roles, if we are looking for people, it will be apparent.

6) Hypnos does not advocate the views of it's community.

7) Please do not share personal information of yourself or others.

8) Do not argue with decisions, if you would like change please notify @NotCreative#9884 on Discord.

9) We require members to be active on the server for at least an hour every two weeks, exceptions can be made to accommodate special circumstances.

10) Please follow the Discord Official Guidelines and please be kind to others.
